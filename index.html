Solutions

Cloud Emulation

Run the cloud locally to accelerate development and improve the developer experience for your team.

Cloud Independence

Remain independent of your cloud provider with our abstractions so your team can plug and play different cloud solutions, enabling them to find the best technology for their problem quickly.

Powerful Integrations

Run our cloud stack in your CI/CD pipelines to save on infrastructure costs and improve your testing process.

Benefits

Save

  • Save costs by avoiding creating cloud resources during the development cycle that are forgotten about and accumulating surprising and unpredictable expenses.
  • Increase developer productivity by reducing the wait time for complex cloud deployments during the product development cycle.
  • Protect your team from security and data issues by providing a local sandbox for developers, so they don't accidentally make changes to production data or grant access to the wrong environment while developing.

Quality

  • Build confidence in the quality of your product using the testable implementations of cloud services we have constructed. Our testable implementations enable your team to test the system in CI/CD pipelines without needing to stand up any external infrastructure.
  • Empower your team to quickly find bugs by sharing the state of their local cloud with other developers.
  • Declutter your cloud by allowing developers to avoid creating new resources by enabling them to work locally.

Speed

  • Prevent wasted time configuring cloud services and waiting for code to deploy; instead, close the feedback loop with a local cloud instance.
  • Avoid creating custom APIs and abstractions for various cloud-based libraries and use our platform-agnostic library. Our APIs allow you to swap different cloud providers to find what works best for your team.
  • Improve developer experience by giving them the power to develop locally and quickly.
